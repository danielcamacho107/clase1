# Catowls

## What are catowls?

Catowls are the ***cutest*** *hybrid*[^1] between a **cat** :cat: and an **owl** :owl:.

They have the body of ~~a bird~~ an owl and the face of a cat.

[^1]: hybrid := a mix between animals.
